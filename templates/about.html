Our Story & Mission

Understanding mental health through compassion, science, and technology

About Insightify

Insightify is a platform dedicated to helping individuals gain a better understanding of their mental health. Our depression self-assessment test is based on the Patient Health Questionnaire-9 (PHQ-9), a scientifically validated tool for screening depression.

Our team of mental health professionals and developers

Insightify emerged from the recognition that many people face barriers when seeking mental health support. Our goal is to bridge this gap by providing accessible tools that can help individuals take the first step toward mental wellness.

Our Mission

We aim to empower individuals by providing a simple and confidential way to assess their mental health. Depression can be difficult to recognize, and we believe that early self-assessment can help people take the first step toward support and recovery.

Through accessible technology and evidence-based approaches, we strive to reduce stigma around mental health and make well-being a priority for everyone.

Science-Based

Our assessment is based on clinically validated questionnaires used by healthcare professionals worldwide.

Privacy First

Your data remains confidential. We prioritize your privacy and security in every aspect of our service.

Accessible to All

Mental health resources should be available to everyone, regardless of their background or location.

How It Works

  1. Answer 9 simple questions based on common depression symptoms.
  2. Receive an immediate result indicating whether you may be experiencing depressive symptoms.
  3. If needed, get recommendations for seeking professional help.

Note: This test is not a substitute for professional diagnosis. If you're struggling, please consult a mental health professional.

Your Privacy Matters

At Insightify, we value your privacy. Your responses are not stored, shared, or used for any purpose other than providing an immediate assessment.

We adhere to strict data protection regulations and implement industry-standard security measures to ensure your information remains confidential.
